How does the 2nd-5th place perk work?

The code didn't arrive — now what?
(1) Check Spam / Promotions. (2) Give it 1-2 minutes. (3) Hit "Resend" on the verification screen. (4) If it's still missing, go back and try a different email.
Where do I find a promo code?
Official launch events, partner campaigns, and friends' referral links can all generate a code (e.g. ABC123). It's optional — you can pre-register without one.

Closed alpha runs in the first half of 2026. Full launch follows later the same year. Pre-registrants get first notice and the first alpha invites.
How is this different from Character.AI or Replika?
Three things: (1) memory that actually persists and surfaces on its own, not just a 4k-token sliding window. (2) Characters are branches of a simulation world, with a real history — not just a prompt-card. (3) Visual novel mode layers scenes, expressions, and voice on top of the conversation.
