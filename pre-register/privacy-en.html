Privacy Policy

Effective date · 2026-04-22 · Pre-release draft

Lucive (the "Company") values your personal information. This policy explains the minimum information collected during pre-registration, its purpose and retention period, and your rights as a user.

1. Personal Information Collected

TypeItemMethod
RequiredEmail addressPre-registration form
OptionalPromo / referral codePre-registration form
OptionalSurvey responses (interests, experience, age range — 8 questions)Core user survey
AutomaticIP, browser info, cookies, access timeAuto-generated on service use

2. Purpose of Collection

  • Pre-registration management · Identifying registrants and preventing duplicates
  • Launch and benefit announcements · Alpha launch, Top 5 ranking, and promo code results via email
  • Core tester guidance · Activity guide and special benefits based on pre-release survey responses
  • Anti-abuse · Detection of automation, fake accounts, and abnormal referral patterns
  • Service improvement · Usage analytics (aggregated, non-identifying)

3. Retention & Usage Period

  • Pre-registration data · Retained for 90 days after alpha launch ends, then deleted
  • If marketing consent is given · Until opt-out or account withdrawal
  • Survey responses · Up to 180 days after selection review ends (for statistical aggregation only)
  • If required by law, retained for the legally mandated period and then deleted

4. Third-Party Disclosure

The Company in principle does not disclose your personal information externally, except in the following cases.

  • When the user has given prior consent
  • When legally requested for investigative purposes through proper procedures

5. Processing Outsourcing

For smooth service operation, the following tasks are outsourced. Processors handle personal information under the Company's supervision.

ProcessorTask
Email delivery service (planned)Welcome / launch / benefit notification emails
Cloud hosting (planned)Data storage and retention
Analytics tools (planned)Access statistics (no PII)

6. User Rights

  • Access, correction, deletion · Request anytime by email
  • Stop processing · Request to halt processing of your personal information
  • Unsubscribe · "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of marketing emails or contact email

7. Cookies

This website uses localStorage and cookies for access convenience and pre-registration state retention. You can disable them anytime in browser settings.

8. Security Measures

  • HTTPS encryption in transit
  • Minimized administrator access with audit logging
  • Regular training for personnel with access to personal information
